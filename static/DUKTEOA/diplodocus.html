Sleep

It was late at night, maybe 11 or 12pm, a Saturday. Louis fiddled with the caulking around the window sill. Sam urged him on, he was cold. Canada in the winter is no place for homeless orphans. Of course they were once in an orphanege, but with the state of the economy they were better off on the street.

"I got it" Louis said.

"Finally, lets get in already!" Sam said, through chattering teeth.
