Each verb has an unbound vowel. Depending on which vowel is placed within it, the meaning of the word changes as follows:

Vowel Meaning
i Verb itself.
e Noun abstracting verb.
a Person doing the verb.
u Adverb of the verb.
o Adjective of the verb.
iqi Location of the noun form.
eqe Believer or practicer of noun form.

All shmigan pronouns begin with an 'i' consonant and end in x. They are made plural the same way that any other word is made plural. Same goes for possessive. Reflexivity is made by using rx instead of x at the end.
